Pancakes

Description

These pancakes are light and fluffy! They make for a great weekend breakfast with your friends or family.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • Almond Milk
  • Oil
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Syrup*
  • Butter or plant based spread

Instructions

  1. Add dry ingredients to one bowl and wet ingredients to another
  2. Whisk wet ingredients together
  3. Pour wet ingredients into the dry and mix until a batter forms
  4. Let batter sit for a few minutes until small bubbles form
  5. Heat a pan to medium heat and pour batter onto pan in circles
  6. When bubbles form and edges appear golden flip pancake and cook until done
  7. Serve with syrup and butter if desired.
