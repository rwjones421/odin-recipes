Coffee

Description

This is a simple french press coffee recipe. Feel free to use whichever roast or beans you prefer!

Ingredients

  • Ground Coffee
  • Water
  • Sugar*
  • Creamer*

Instructions

  1. Boil water in kettle or pot
  2. Put coffee in french press
  3. Pour hot water over coffee grounds, ensuring all are wet
  4. Let french press sit for 4 minutes
  5. Pour coffee into mug, add sugar or creamer as desired.
